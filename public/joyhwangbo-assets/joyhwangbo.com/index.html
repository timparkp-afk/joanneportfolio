human writing
for real humans

As an NYC-based copywriter leading strategy and storytelling, I write conversational copy that converts.

At Justworks, I led copy for nationwide brand campaigns. I managed the social media and editorial team at Clove to help the brand go viral with 9.2M impressions and become the #1 award-winning sneaker in healthcare.

Currently, I’m leading copy for Teladoc Health and increased brand awareness by 47%, the highest in company history.

I’ve owned language across every single channel, including paid/organic social, programmatic, video, email, SMS, out-of-home ads, product, and website.
